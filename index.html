JSID.DEV

Free subdomain for developers.

.jsid.dev

jsid.dev is a free identity for developers on the web. This service offers a handy subdomain service for you to have a subdomain like foo.jsid.dev or bar.jsid.dev for use on your static sites via CNAME DNS record.


Fork, add subdomain and send a pull request. We will make it work!
